JavaScript Notbook

JavaScript Notebook is a site with a collection of exercises and solutions to test your knowledge on this subject. Here you will find an excellent way to practice with a view to a technical test You will also find theoretical explanations and suggestions to learn this programming language in the shortest possible time and in the simplest way. I will leave the more technical concepts to more specialized sites that I will also suggest to you The presentation has been made, you just need to get down to work. My suggestion: If you are just starting out, I recommend entrusting your guide to FreeCodeCamp, if you already have experience, then challenge yourself to complete each of the challenges on this site. You will never be stuck because I leave you the possible solutions, yes, always give yourself many opportunities to try.

JavaScript Overview

Javascript is an object-oriented programming language. It is one of the languages ​​most used by FrontEnd developers to streamline the interfaces of a web application.

Study material

There are numerous sources through which you can get a solid foundation of this language and be able to go deeper over time. However, a lot of information sometimes tends to lose us and leads us to get frustrated in the process. A guided learning from the simplest to the most complex is what is always recommended.

FreeCodeCamp It is a website of a non-profit collective of interactive teaching accessible to everyone, with which you can get a lot of information and exercises on this and other languages. Its system is very interactive so you will learn by practicing

Something very positive is also that he gives you a certificate at the end of his course.
